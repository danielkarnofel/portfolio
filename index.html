About Me

Daniel Karnofel is interested in exploring new perceptual and conceptual modalities created by accelerating technological progress. The crafting and sharing of virtual worlds and experiences allows for a new kind of discourse that both includes and supercedes what is possible with traditional mediums. His work aims to represent spatial and aesthetic dimensions that are outside of typcial human expererience by using technology as both a filter and guide for revealing new phenomenological frontiers.
Daniel has a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy and a Minor in Art and Technology from the University of California, Santa Barbara.
